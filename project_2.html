Nutanix Private Cloud &
TrueNAS Deployment

Project Details

Client: Registrar General’s Department
Location: Registrar Office Data Center
Period: April 2025

1. Project Overview

This project involved the modernization of the Registrar General’s Department data center through the deployment of a Hyperconverged Infrastructure (HCI). The primary goal was to implement a Nutanix Private Cloud solution to integrate computing, storage, and networking into a single, scalable platform. Additionally, the project included the deployment of TrueNAS to provide robust, dedicated storage management alongside the HCI environment.

2. Technical Scope

The technical focus was on establishing a unified IT infrastructure capable of handling enterprise workloads with high availability and resilience.

Hyperconverged Infrastructure (HCI): Utilization of Nutanix to merge storage, compute, and networking, simplifying data center operations.
Cloud Management: Implementation of Nutanix core components (AOS, Prism, and AHV) to manage the private cloud environment.
Storage Solutions: Deployment of TrueNAS for efficient, software-defined storage management.

3. My Contributions

Phase 1: Nutanix HCI Deployment

  • Deployment Initiation: Commenced the deployment of the Nutanix infrastructure at the Registrar Office Data Center.
  • System Configuration: Configured the Nutanix environment, setting up necessary parameters for the unified platform.
  • Testing & Validation: Conducted initial testing to verify stability and functionality.

Phase 2: TrueNAS Storage Deployment

  • Storage Setup: Initiated the deployment of the TrueNAS storage solution.
  • Configuration & Testing: Configured TrueNAS to meet storage requirements and performed functionality tests.

4. Key Learnings Applied

Gained practical experience with Nutanix AOS, Prism, and AHV, understanding how they enable the management of private and hybrid cloud environments through a single interface. Developed a deeper understanding of how HCI reduces physical footprint while improving performance and flexibility compared to traditional infrastructure.
